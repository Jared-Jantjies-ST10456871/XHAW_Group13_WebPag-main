About Us

Empower The Nation is a Johannesburg-based SME that was established in 2018. We are dedicated to offering essential skills development courses to help individuals grow in their personal and professional lives. Our goal is to provide comprehensive, hands-on learning experiences in various fields, ensuring that our learners are equipped with the practical knowledge and confidence to succeed.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To empower individuals with practical skills that promote independence, career growth, and community development. We strive to create a society where everyone has access to the tools and knowledge needed to thrive.

Mission: Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable courses in essential skills like Child Minding, First Aid, Sewing, Cooking, Landscaping, Gardening Maintenance, and Life Skills. Through these courses, we aim to uplift and support the growth of our community.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Empower The Nation began with the goal of filling a gap in the skills development market in Johannesburg. Over the past few years, we have grown from a small startup to a trusted provider of practical courses, serving hundreds of individuals across the city. Our expansion into new areas like First Aid and Landscaping reflects our commitment to meeting the diverse needs of our community.

Our Courses

Child Minding

A 6-week course designed to equip learners with essential childcare skills, including safety, nutrition, and developmental activities for young children.

Cooking

Master the basics of cooking with our practical course. Learn how to prepare delicious and healthy meals for families or commercial settings in just 6 weeks.

Gardening Maintenance

This 6-week course covers essential gardening techniques, including plant care, garden design, and maintenance to help you create and maintain beautiful outdoor spaces.

First Aid

A vital skill for everyone! Our First Aid course teaches emergency response and basic medical care in critical situations.

Landscaping

Learn how to design and maintain outdoor landscapes with professional guidance in this comprehensive course.

Sewing

Our Sewing course covers both the basics and advanced sewing techniques, preparing you for personal projects or a career in tailoring.

Life Skills

This course focuses on essential life skills, such as communication, time management, and problem-solving, to help learners excel in both personal and professional settings.
